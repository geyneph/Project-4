About B safe


Cancer survival overall predictor Model

We introduce the machine learning model named BSafe, designed to predict the probability of breast cancer survival while considering multiple clinical and molecular variables. B Safe addresses the need to improve breast cancer prognosis, one of the leading causes of mortality among women worldwide. This innovative approach integrates artificial intelligence techniques and data analysis to analyze factors such as patient age, tumor size, mutation count, cellularity level, and specific breast cancer type. By providing fast and personalized results, BSafe empowers medical professionals and patients with relevant information for informed clinical decision-making. Additionally, this model can make a valuable contribution to medical research by facilitating the analysis of large datasets and identifying predictive patterns. Ultimately, BSafe represents a significant advancement in improving breast cancer care and prognosis, with the potential to positively impact patient quality of life and long-term health outcomes.
